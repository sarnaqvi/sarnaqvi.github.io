Sarah Naqvi is an undergraduate flute student at Berklee College of Music, majoring in Electronic Production and Design. She hopes to pursue a career in music cognition research, blending her passions for music and science. Sarah is particularly interested in Pan-Asian music and non-western musical traditions. She is excited about incorporating these topics into her future research.
